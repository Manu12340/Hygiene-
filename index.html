health-hygiene

Health hygiene plays a vital role in promoting wellness and preventing the spread of diseases. It encompasses a range of practices that individuals can adopt to maintain cleanliness and preserve their overall well-being. From simple habits like handwashing to more comprehensive measures such as environmental cleanliness and vaccination, health hygiene acts as a powerful shield against various illnesses. This essay explores the significance of health hygiene and its impact on personal and public health.
